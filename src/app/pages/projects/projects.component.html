Smart Home Automation

The Anywhere Smart Home Automation System is an ESP32-based IoT solution that enhances energy efficiency and safety by automating appliances and detecting motion, gas leaks, and fire. It supports both manual and automatic control modes via the Blynk app and physical buttons, ensuring real-time alerts and reliable performance.
